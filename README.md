## Project Overview

CleanCoast Cape Town is a non-profit organisation focused on marine conservation and beach cleanup initiatives in Cape Town, South Africa. This website was developed to raise awareness about ocean pollution, recruit volunteers for cleanup events, and collect donations to support conservation efforts.

### Purpose of the Website
The primary goal of this website is to provide information about the organisation's mission, showcase the impact of cleanup efforts through before/after images, and encourage community involvement through volunteer sign-ups and donations.

### Target Audience
- Local residents of Cape Town who want to volunteer
- Schools and educational institutions
- Tourists who want to give back to the community
- Corporate sponsors looking for environmental initiatives

---

## Website Goals and Objectives

| Goal | Objective |
|------|-----------|
| Increase volunteer sign-ups | Recruit 50 new volunteers per month through online form |
| Raise awareness | Educate visitors about plastic pollution impact |
| Generate donations | Raise R10,000 for equipment and cleanup events |
| Showcase impact | Display before/after cleanup photos to demonstrate results |

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Number of volunteer sign-ups submitted via form
- Donation amount raised (tracked against R10,000 goal)
- Website page views and time spent on site
- Click-through rate on "Volunteer Today" buttons
