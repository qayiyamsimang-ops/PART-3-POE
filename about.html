About Us

Our Mission

We aim to create a sustainable, clean, and beautiful coastline for Cape Town. Every cleanup event brings us closer to a future where plastic pollution is something we read about, not walk on.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by local surfers and volunteers, CleanCoast Cape Town began with a simple idea: small actions make big waves. What started as a WhatsApp group sharing cleanup dates is now a community-wide initiative supported by schools, students, and local businesses.

Our first major cleanup in 2022 collected over 300kg of trash from Muizenberg Beach. Since then, we've grown to over 500 volunteers and have removed thousands of kilograms of plastic from Cape Town's coastline.

Our Team

🌊 Sarah Jones - Founder & Director

+

Sarah is a marine biologist with over 10 years of experience in ocean conservation. She founded CleanCoast Cape Town after seeing the devastating effects of plastic pollution on her local beach. Sarah holds a Masters in Marine Biology from the University of Cape Town and has published several papers on plastic pollution in South African waters.
